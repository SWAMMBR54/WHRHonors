Science Olympiad

I've been in Science Olympiad since Middle School, and I am a very active member at Oak Grove. While we didn't win any major awards last year, we still got to go to the State Competion (which was held at USM!) and had a blast! Science has always been one of my favorite subjects, and so it's amazing that I get to learn about a variety of interesting topics, and then get to test my knowledge against other kids my age!

Dungeons & Dragons

For the past 6 years, I've been an avid player of D&D. It's one of my favorite pastimes, whether it be because of the exciting adventures, strategic combat, intriguing roleplaying/worldbuilding, or simply just lighthearted shenanigans between players. I serve as the Dungeon Master for my current group, which is a lot of fun, because I now get to share my love of the game with my players (who are all close friends of mine!)

Gaming

I enjoy a wide variety of video games, whether they be FPS', Strategy, Co-op, RPGs, or even party games! I've been lucky to have a friend group that loves gaming just as much as I do, and so I always have someone to turn to when I'm feeling bored. Currently, my favorite game is Rare's Sea of Thieves, a popular piratical online shared-world adventure game.
